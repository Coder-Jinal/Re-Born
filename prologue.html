Prologue

Elias: “My name is Elias, and I've got an important job to do. I'm a spiritual medium, meaning that I can talk with the dead. I've been helping the remnants of people move on from this world, so their spirits can rest in peace. Today, I'm at Ridgefield Manor. There used to be a lot of people living in it, but that was a long time ago… I've heard some rumors of something haunting these halls, so I've come to help whoever is trapped here.”
